Background

I’m a self-taught front-end developer who loves everything about creating content. I began my journey into tech as a photographer, videographer and graphic designer creating websites, graphics and media for small businesses.

After nearly a decade of working in aviation, I decided to make the formal transition into tech. I’m excited to bring my life experiences to the process of building fantastic looking websites and useful apps.

Goals

Short-Term

Secure a remote entry-level front end developer position with a company that will support my continued education and growth. Learn, learn and learn!

Mid-Range

There are so many niches and specialties that I have yet to explore. A few areas that have piqued my interest are blockchain technology, backend programming, machine learning, and iOS development.

Long-term

Professionally, I hope to make significant contributions to the tech community. Whether that is creating a new language or improving upon an existing one. Personally, I plan to start a nonprofit to expose children to the vast field of tech, teach them to code and mentor those who want to pursue tech careers.
